Full Self-Driving, Skynet and other AI Myths

The forthcoming book by Brad Flaugher. I hope this book helps demystify the world of deep learning as I understand it. I discuss, and sometimes answer the following questions:

💩 What progress in AI is revolutionary and what is bullshit?
✨ What is AI good for? What kind of datasets can we use?
🎨 Can AI create new things well? Can it innovate by itself?
🕵️ Is my company and job at risk? Is this use of AI ethical?
☢️Can it be stopped?
