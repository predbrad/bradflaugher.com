Is the bootcamp right for me?

The bootcamp is right for you if you have an interest in data and a desire to learn, previous programming experience in any language is ideal but not strictly required. Applicants will be judged primarily on their drive and willingness to succeed in a career as a data engineer.

What happens next?

After your application is has been reviewed you will get an email (usually in under 2 weeks), if accepted you will be sent the bootcamp contract with payment terms and expectations. you may email apply@bradflaugher.com with any questions.

How much does it cost?

It depends on how good of a job you land after class. If you get a job for $100k(USD) annual salary or over you'll pay full price for the bootcamp, which is about $10k, if you get a job between $80k-100k you'll pay a prorated rate depending on your pay. If you land a job that pays under $80k you'll pay only about $2k, which covers the cost of your new laptop that you'll be given and the cloud computing credits.

We aim to maintain competitive pricing, and research salaries as well as competitor bootcamps regularly, we are happy to share this reseach to help you make a decision to attend or not.
